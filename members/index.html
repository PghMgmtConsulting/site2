Join Our Team

Welcome to Pittsburgh Management Consulting (PMC), the University of Pittsburgh’s premier student-led consulting group. Here at PMC, our members collaborate with Fortune 500 companies and local Pittsburgh businesses to craft innovative solutions for real-world business challenges. Each project team, consisting of six to eight students, engages in semester-long collaborations. Within these teams, you'll find Analysts, Consultants, and Project Managers, each playing a crucial role with unique time commitments and responsibilities.
